I am a first-year CS PhD student at the University of Michigan advised by Prof. Nima Fazeli and Prof. JJ Park. Previously, I was a student researcher at Georgia Tech advised by Prof. Danfei Xu. I also worked with Prof. Yilun Du at Harvard University, and I received my M.S. in CS from Brown University where I worked with Prof. Chen Sun. Before that, I worked with Prof. Lin Wang at NTU Singapore and Prof. Jianguo Zhang at SUSTech on computer vision.

My research goal is to develop intelligent agents that can handle daily chores. My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and robotics.
