Self-Evolving Neural Radiance Fields

Jaewoo Jung*, Jisang Han*, Jiwon Kang*, Seongchan Kim, Min-Seop Kwak, Seungryong Kim

ArXiv Preprint, 2023

Abstract

We propose a novel framework, dubbed Self-Evolving Neural Radiance Fields (SE-NeRF), that applies a self-training framework to NeRF to address these problems. We formulate few-shot NeRF into a teacher-student framework to guide the network to learn a more robust representation of the scene by training the student with additional pseudo labels generated from the teacher.

GeCoNeRF: Few-shot Neural Radiance Fields via Geometric Consistency

Min-Seop Kwak*, Jiuhn Song*, Seungryong Kim

International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) 2023

Project Page
Abstract

We present a novel framework to regularize Neural Radiance Field (NeRF) in a few-shot setting with a geometry-aware consistency regularization. The proposed approach leverages a rendered depth map at unobserved viewpoint to warp sparse input images to the unobserved viewpoint and impose them as pseudo ground truths to facilitate learning of NeRF.
